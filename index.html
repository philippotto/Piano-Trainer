Welcome to pitch training!

When you hit Start, notes will be displayed in the stave above. Just use the mini claviature below to play the notes. Don't worry about the rhythm or speed for now.

The generated notes will be so that you play only one note at a time. If you want to practice chords, have a look into the Set Up section to hook up your digital piano.

Welcome to rhythm training!
